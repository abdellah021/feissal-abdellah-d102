INAUGURATION OF TWO AMPHITHEATRES

Sanday 20 Mars 2020


The international joint AMIR laboratory of the Faculty of Sciences of Rabat organized a workshop on responsible mining activity under the theme: Responsible mining activity, Issues, challenges and solutions in the province of Jerada; and this from December 1 to 3, 2021 at the Mohamed Premier University of Oujda. This workshop brought together 40 participants from multiple backgrounds: researchers, teacher-researchers, political decision-makers, provincial services and consultancies involved in the thematic relating to the province, and who are authors of the main published works from different disciplinary fields relating to the physical and engineering sciences, life sciences, humanities and social sciences. The first objective of this event was to adopt a participatory approach with a view to developing a state of the art of scientific knowledge on mining activity in the region since the start of operations. Next, this workshop will draw up a roadmap for interdisciplinary and transdisciplinary action research, responding to the various issues widely raised, in response to current societal demands expressed by community partners and in the light of research already carried out. Finally, a field trip was offered to the participants who went to the Jerada mining site and to the project site of the mining museum park under development in this city.
